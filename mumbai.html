Top Tourist Attractions in Mumbai

Gateway of India

A historic monument built during the British Raj. It is an iconic symbol of Mumbai and an architectural marvel standing at the edge of the Arabian Sea.

Visit ♥

Marine Drive

Also known as the ‘Queen’s Necklace’, Marine Drive is a 3.6 km long promenade along the Arabian Sea, offering breathtaking views of the coastline, especially during the evening.

Visit ♥

Elephanta Caves

Located on Elephanta Island, these ancient rock-cut caves are known for their intricate sculptures and temples dedicated to Hindu gods. A UNESCO World Heritage site.

Visit ♥

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus

One of Mumbai's busiest railway stations, this Victorian Gothic architectural gem has been declared a UNESCO World Heritage site due to its stunning design and historical significance.

Visit ♥
